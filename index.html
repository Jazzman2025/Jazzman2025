About Me

Welcome to my musical world! I am an experienced saxophonist with a deep passion for improvisation, bringing vibrant energy to every performance. Over the years, I have had the privilege of playing in various settings, from lively clubs and bars to inspiring church bands. My journey on the saxophone has been fueled by a love for creating spontaneous melodies and connecting with audiences through music. Whether I'm jamming with fellow musicians or performing solo, my goal is to evoke emotion and invite listeners on an unforgettable musical journey. Join me as we explore the limitless possibilities of sound together! My passion and strong desire are to help fellow musicians build confidence in their improvisation skills and truly enjoy playing their instruments.If you're interested in starting your saxophone journey or know someone who is, getting in touch is easy! You can reach me via email at stevenreedmusic@gmail.com or by phone at 07407129611. I’m happy to answer any questions you may have about the lessons, whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refresh your skills. Additionally, Feel free to connect with me on social media via WhatsApp and Messenger. Let’s arrange a time for your first lesson and embark on this exciting musical adventure together!
